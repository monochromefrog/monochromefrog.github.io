About

My name is Dyota Pratyaksa, I'm a self-taught Illustrator from Indonesia. I love creating graphic for apparel and merchandise design such as T-shirts, posters, album covers, book covers, etc. Most of the time, animals are my drawing subject; drawn in cartoony (comic-like) style with decorative elements. Other than animals, I also draw skulls, people, and pop culture related stuff.

Coffee, mecha model kits, and video games are my other interests. I occasionally write blog article around my drawing experience—but not much—and also giving out freebies, so be sure to check them out.

I go by the name monochromefrog on various social media if you feel like checking them out. Thanks for visiting my website and enjoy your visit.

Regards,

Dyota
