Get To Know

I’m Dyota Pratyaksa, a self-taught illustrator from Semarang, Indonesia. I do illustrations for apparel, prints, and screen purpose projects. My illustrations ranged from cartoon to semi-realistic (comic-like) look. I’m open to any kind of style, but I tend to work with heavy ink line details and minimal colors as my standard.

When I’m not working on illustration projects, I do other stuff like layout designs for web and prints, blog writing, and English to Indonesian translation/localization. Head over to Contact page if you want to know more. Thank you for reading this.
